About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher under the supervision of Prof. Jianhong Wu at York University, Canada. I received my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from the University of Western Ontario, working with Prof. Xingfu Zou.

My research interests include:

  • Mathematical biology
  • Infectious disease modelling
  • Dynamical systems
  • Delay differential equations

Recent Work

My work explores the mathematical foundations and applications of nonlinear dynamics in epidemiology. I study mathematical structures behind transmission dynamics, stability phenomena, bifurcations in delay systems, and mechanisms that drive disease persistence or elimination.
